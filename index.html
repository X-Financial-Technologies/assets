XFT MUNICIPAL INCOME FUND

SUMMARY PROSPECTUS

This summary prospectus for the XFT Municipal Income Fund (the “Fund”) provides a concise overview of key information. It does not contain all of the information that you should consider before investing in the Fund’s shares. Please read the complete Prospectus and Statement of Additional Information (“SAI”), especially the information under “Risk Factors,” for a more detailed discussion of the Fund’s strategies, operations, and associated risks.

The Fund XFT Municipal Income Fund is a continuously offered, diversified, closed-end management investment company registered under the Investment Company Act of 1940 (“1940 Act”). The Fund intends to operate as an “interval fund,” making repurchase offers for 5% to 25% of its outstanding shares at net asset value (“NAV”) on a quarterly basis, unless postponed or suspended under regulatory guidelines.
Common Shares The Fund’s common shares of beneficial interest, called “Common Shares,” represent an ownership interest in the Fund. Holders of Common Shares are referred to as “Common Shareholders.”

Investment Objective The Fund seeks to provide current income exempt from federal personal income taxes while maintaining liquidity and a stable share price of $1.
Investment Policies &
Restrictions		 Under normal market conditions, at least 80% of the Fund’s Managed Assets will be invested in municipal securities that XFT deems to be of investment grade quality. The Fund may invest up to 20% of its Managed Assets in municipal securities rated below investment grade (“junk bonds”). The Fund maintains a dollar-weighted average maturity of 60 days or less and a dollar-weighted average life of 120 days or less. The Fund may concentrate 25% or more of its Managed Assets in securities of issuers located in the same state or economic sector.

The Fund’s fundamental investment restrictions, as detailed in the Prospectus and SAI, may only be changed by a vote of a majority of the outstanding voting securities. No assurance can be given that the Fund’s investment objective or strategies will be achieved.
Principal Strategies The Fund focuses on high-quality, short-term municipal securities with effective maturities of 397 days or less, aiming to maintain minimal credit risk through maturity and diversification considerations. The Fund invests in municipal obligations that generate interest exempt from federal income taxes.
Management & Organization Investment Advisor: X Financial Technologies (“XFT”). XFT oversees the Fund’s day-to-day management, including security selection and compliance with the Fund’s investment strategies. The Advisor’s compensation is based on the Fund’s Managed Assets.

The Advisor’s investment team members have extensive experience managing municipal bond portfolios and short-duration strategies. Additional details regarding the Advisor’s background and compensation structure can be found in the Fund’s Prospectus and SAI.
Shareholder Information Interval Fund Repurchases: The Fund will conduct quarterly repurchase offers for 5–25% of outstanding shares at NAV. Notices are sent at least 21 days before each repurchase request deadline.

Purchases & Redemptions: Shares may be purchased or redeemed at https://xft.finance. The minimum initial investments are $3,000 for Dollar Shares and $50,000 for DeFi Shares; subsequent investments require $1. If investing through an intermediary, please contact that firm directly for eligibility and purchase/redemption procedures.

Dividend Reinvestment: Unless otherwise elected, distributions are automatically reinvested in additional shares. Shareholders may elect to receive cash instead, or revoke that election at any time.
Tax Information A significant portion of the Fund’s distributions is expected to be exempt from federal personal income taxes. However, certain distributions may be subject to state or local taxes, or to the federal alternative minimum tax. For detailed information on the tax treatment of Fund distributions, refer to “Tax Matters” in the Prospectus and SAI.
